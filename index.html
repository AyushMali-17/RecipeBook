Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients: Spaghetti, Eggs, Parmesan, Pancetta, Pepper

Instructions: Boil pasta, cook pancetta, mix with eggs and cheese, combine with pasta.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients: Chicken, Curry powder, Coconut milk, Onions, Garlic

Instructions: Cook onions and garlic, add chicken, add curry powder and coconut milk, simmer.

Beef Stew

Ingredients: Beef, Potatoes, Carrots, Onions, Beef broth

Instructions: Brown beef, add vegetables and broth, simmer until tender.
